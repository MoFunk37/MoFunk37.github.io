Jon Baglo - Multi-Instrumentalist/Composer

Friendship 7 - Neon High - Men Against the Sea

Jon Baglo has been writing, recording, and producing music for over 25 years. His first "claim to fame" was the semi-viral video of his cover of Boston's Foreplay/Long Time in June of 2009. Since then he's performed on a number of works including drums (and the occasional bass) with Men Against the Sea, and all the instrumentation with Neon High and Friendship 7.
